Who is Coach Kim?

Currently

  • President of Kim, Hyung-Tak Archery Training Center
  • Member of World archery coach committee for 12 years
  • Chariman of the Coach Committee of Korea

Coaching career

  • 1967: Began archery
  • 1970: Started as archery coach
  • 1984: National team coach of Korean women's team for the LA Olympics
  • 1985: National team coach of Korean women's team for Seoul World Championship
  • 1986: National team coach of Korean women's team for Seoul Asian games
  • 1990: Head coach for Taiwan national team for Beijing Asian Games
  • 1991: Head coach for Taiwan national team for Poland World Championship Competition
  • 1992: Head coach for Taiwan national team for Barcelona Olympics
  • 1993: Head coach for Taiwan's national team for Turkey World Championship competition
  • 1994: Head coach for Taiwan's national team for Hiroshima Asian Games
  • 2015: National team coach of Universiade game of Korean team

Published materials

  • Archery book - Kim, Hyung-Tak Archery
  • Archery book - Korean teaching manual
  • Participated in creating the World archery coaching manual
